(1) High Volume Rate: RVnGOs rental fee to owners is 8% on the first $2,000 of bookings per month (or $160 in fees) and then it’s 3% for the remainder of the month. If an owner has more than one rental RV, they can average over the fleet, so if you have 10 RVs, when your fleet reaches $20,000 in bookings, you pay only 3% thereafter. Yes, that’s right, and you don’t have to pay your own credit card transaction fee, we will do that for you (and not coincidentally our credit card processing cost is 3% - we’re not that crazy). (2) Demand Aggregation: RVnGO and other listings sites help aggregate people searching for a product, so they don’t have to visit individual web sites one after another, and give them a single interface to work with. Your listing benefits from this. (3) Marketing Aggregation: We market for you, bringing RVers looking for RVs to our site so they can find yours! (4) Dedicated Owner URL Page: Each owner has their own inventory page which shows all their RVs for rent and for sale. When a user clicks on the owner icon on any search result, they are taken to your page where they will see your other inventory. You can customize this page with your own background, and better yet when you register your URLs or domains with us, any user that comes through your URL will only see your inventory and not anyone else’s (whether they came through to your full listing inventory or an individual listing), but your inventory will still be available to the broader site to attract new bookings!
